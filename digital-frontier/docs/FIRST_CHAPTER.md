# First Adventure Chapter

Polished opening of Digital Frontier — Grassland only. No new biomes.

## Player fantasy

Boot → partner → Home → Adventure → Park life → trail mystery → Alpha → Pine Hollow sanctum → Warden → chapter clear → **first evolution** → “I want to know what happens next.”

## Spine (connected)

| # | Quest | What you do |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | First Steps | Welcome sign · chest · Park Guide |
| 2 | Grassland Call | Meet Field Ranger |
| 3 | Pine Threat | Trail cache · Fuel Clerk prep · defeat **Glitch Alpha** |
| 4 | Hollow Challenge | Discover Hollow · open **Root Gate** · defeat **Hollow Warden** |
| 5 | Clear | Title · Bits · town celebration · growth ceremony |

Side quests drip afterward on the **same corridor** (Injured Signal, Lost Trail, etc.).

## Pine Hollow Sanctum

First major location (outdoor dungeonette at the existing landmark):

- Approach corridor + lore plaque  
- **Root Gate** (opens after Alpha)  
- West / East **Root Seals**  
- Arena ring + rare chest  
- Warden set piece (phases + unique moves)

## Guidance

Cyan ◆ beacon (`ChapterGuidance`) points toward the active MAIN objective. Field Unit chrome shows the quest line.

## Opening

First deploy: title card + short Field Unit mystery dialogue.

## Living town

Park Kid + Park Elder roam; Fuel Clerk / Guide / Ranger react to quest stage; celebration banners after clear; reputation flag `reputation_park_hero`.

## First evolution

Chapter paths (`*_chapter_one.tres`) unlock on `chapter_grassland_cleared`. Ceremony plays after clear.

## World reactivity

NPC memory/disposition, story beats, gate/seal flags, post-clear dialogue, celebration props.
