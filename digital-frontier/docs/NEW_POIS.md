## New this pass

### 1. Mirror Mere (inspired by central lake POIs)

| Trait | Reference feeling | Digital Frontier take |
|-------|-------------------|------------------------|
| Theme | Lake as map heart | Quiet reflective mere — “bits shimmer on the water” |
| Landmark | Island in water | Pixel island cabin + dock ring |
| Layout | Shore houses around lake | 3 shore cottages + boathouse + island cabin (enterable) |
| Atmosphere | Open, scenic, contested mid | Soft water animation, reeds, fishing pier |
| Gameplay | Cross water / island loot | Dock chests, island secret chest, shore discoverables |

**Why here:** Center-north of Grassland (~east of Park, south of Reels road) so travelers between Park↔Reels glimpse water from the highway.

### 2. Market Mile (inspired by retail strip POIs)

| Trait | Reference feeling | Digital Frontier take |
|-------|-------------------|------------------------|
| Theme | One long shopping street | Neon-letter “MILE” strip of original shops |
| Landmark | Big-box / row of stores | Anchor department store + toy / grocery / diner |
| Layout | Parallel shops facing road | N–S asphalt mile, parking bays, loading alley |
| Atmosphere | Colorful, commercial | Bright signs, carts, parked cars |
| Gameplay | Store interiors / back rooms | Enterable shop, alley chest, rooftop access prop |

**Why here:** East of Park / north of Salty — a commercial stop on the way to the farm belt.

### 3. Grease Grove (inspired by fast-food suburb POIs)

| Trait | Reference feeling | Digital Frontier take |
|-------|-------------------|------------------------|
| Theme | Greasy spoon + houses | “GigaBite” drive-thru plaza + 3 bungalows |
| Landmark | Oversized food mascot | Giant pixel burger statue (not franchise IP) |
| Layout | Plaza + suburban ring | Lot, canopy, playground, houses west |
| Atmosphere | Warm, kitschy, evening neon | Orange/red roof accents, lamp glow |
| Gameplay | Kitchen / playground secrets | Enterable diner booth building, playground chest |

**Why here:** West of Pleasant Park — fills the empty west approach toward Storm Prairie expansion.
