## What you can do now

**Home**
- See Pixel Fox’s mood and hunger
- **Feed** / **Play** / **Rest** to care for them
- Mood color changes with how they feel
- **Go on Adventure** (or press Enter)

**Adventure**
- **WASD** or arrows to move
- **H** to go home
